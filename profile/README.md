# SolverForge

**Optimization infrastructure for real-world planning systems.**

SolverForge helps engineering teams turn business constraints into executable software for planning, scheduling, routing, and operational decision-making.

Built for serious optimization work, it combines expressive modeling, precise control over business rules, and production-grade performance in a toolkit designed for complex real-world systems.

## What SolverForge is for

SolverForge is designed for systems where decisions must satisfy hard constraints, reflect business logic, and perform under real operational pressure.

Typical applications include:

- Planning and scheduling
- Routing and dispatch
- Resource allocation
- Constraint-driven operational workflows
- Custom optimization systems embedded in production products

## Point of view

**Write constraints like you write code.**

Optimization software should be explicit, programmable, and engineered with intent — not hidden behind opaque tooling or reduced to brittle manual workflows.

SolverForge exists to make advanced planning systems easier to build, easier to reason about, and easier to ship at a professional standard.
